About Lyndishes

A culinary institute built for the next generation.

A professional culinary institute based in Gwarimpa, Abuja, dedicated to training the next generation of chefs and food entrepreneurs.

Founded by Chef Lyn Ewalefoh, a City & Guilds certified chef trained at the International Centre for Culinary Arts (ICCA), Dubai, Lyndishes was created to bridge the gap between culinary skills and real food business knowledge.

Students receive hands-on training in professional cooking techniques, kitchen management, food presentation, and culinary creativity — all while building the entrepreneurial mindset to grow a profitable food business.

Since its establishment, Lyndishes has trained thousands of students now working as chefs, bakers, caterers, and food business owners across Nigeria.

  • Hands-on professional culinary education
  • Practical pastry, baking, and kitchen management training
  • Entrepreneurship-focused learning for food businesses
  • Built for career growth and profitable opportunities

Meet the Founder

Chef Lyn Ewalefoh

Founder, Lyndishes Culinary School

Chef Lyn Ewalefoh is a passionate culinary professional and educator dedicated to empowering aspiring chefs and food entrepreneurs with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the food industry.

She is a City & Guilds certified chef who trained at the International Centre for Culinary Arts (ICCA), Dubai — one of the world's leading culinary training institutions.

Through Lyndishes, she has trained thousands of students, many of whom have gone on to launch thriving catering services, restaurants, bakeries, and food brands across Nigeria.
