About Lyndishes

A culinary institute built for the next generation.

A professional culinary institute based in Gwarimpa, Abuja, dedicated to training the next generation of chefs and food entrepreneurs across Nigeria.

Founded by Lynda Chinasa Ewalefoh, a City & Guilds certified chef trained at the International Centre for Culinary Arts (ICCA), Dubai, Lyndishes was built to bridge the gap between culinary skill and real food business knowledge.

Students receive hands-on training in professional cooking techniques, kitchen management, food presentation, and entrepreneurial strategy, all while building a profitable food business mindset.

  • Hands-on professional culinary education
  • Pastry, baking & kitchen management training
  • Entrepreneurship-focused business curriculum
  • Career pathways in Nigeria's growing food industry

FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Everything you need to know before enrolling at Lyndishes Culinary School.

Do I need prior cooking experience to enrol?
+
No prior experience is required. Our programmes are designed for complete beginners as well as those with some cooking background. You'll build from fundamentals up to professional-level techniques.
Are the certificates recognised by employers?
+
Yes. LCS certificates are professionally issued and backed by internationally recognised standards. Our graduates have used them to secure positions at hotels, restaurants, and catering firms across Nigeria.
How long do the programmes run?
+
Programme lengths vary. Short courses run 1–4 weeks, while our Professional Diploma spans several months. Visit our Programs page for a full breakdown of each course duration.
Is there a focus on food business / entrepreneurship?
+
Absolutely. Entrepreneurship is woven into every Lyndishes programme. You'll learn food costing, pricing strategy, branding, and how to launch and run a profitable food business alongside your culinary skills.
Where is the school located?
+
Lyndishes Culinary School is located in Gwarimpa, Abuja, Nigeria. Contact us via WhatsApp or email for the precise address and directions before your first class.
How do I apply or register?
+
Click the "Apply Now" button on any page to complete our registration form. You can also reach us directly via WhatsApp on +234 810 140 8693 or email us at culinary.school@lyndishes.com.
