About Lyndishes

A culinary institute built for the next generation.

A professional culinary institute based in Gwarimpa, Abuja, dedicated to training the next generation of chefs and food entrepreneurs across Nigeria.

Founded by Lynda Chinasa Ewalefoh — a City & Guilds certified chef trained at the International Centre for Culinary Arts (ICCA), Dubai — Lyndishes was built to bridge the gap between culinary skill and real food business knowledge.

Students receive hands-on training in professional cooking techniques, kitchen management, food presentation, and entrepreneurial strategy — all while building a profitable food business mindset.

  • Hands-on professional culinary education
  • Pastry, baking & kitchen management training
  • Entrepreneurship-focused business curriculum
  • Career pathways in Nigeria's growing food industry
