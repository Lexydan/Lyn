About Lyndishes

The Fastest Way to Start a Profitable Food Business in Abuja

Lyndishes Culinary School is a professional culinary institute based in Gwarimpa, Abuja. We train the next generation of chefs and food entrepreneurs, not just how to cook, but how to build a business from it.

Founded by Lynda Chinasa Ewalefo, a City & Guilds certified chef trained at the International Centre for Culinary Arts (ICCA) Dubai, the school bridges culinary mastery with real food business knowledge.
