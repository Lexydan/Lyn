About Lyndishes

A Culinary School Built for Real-World Success

Lyndishes Culinary School is a professional culinary institute based in Gwarimpa, Abuja, dedicated to training the next generation of chefs and food entrepreneurs.

Founded by Chef Lyn Chinasa Ewalefo — a City & Guilds certified chef trained at the International Centre for Culinary Arts (ICCA) Dubai — the school bridges the gap between culinary skills and real food business knowledge.

Students receive hands-on training in professional cooking techniques, kitchen management, food presentation, and culinary creativity — plus strong emphasis on entrepreneurship.

  • ✓Hands-on culinary and pastry training
  • ✓Professional kitchen discipline and work ethics
  • ✓Business-focused education for food entrepreneurs
  • ✓Designed to build profitable food careers
